ANOMALY 12.

At 12:34 AM on Wednesday, March 24th, Alpha-e-Omega Squadron encountered an unexpected anomaly while searching for flaws in "serie" number 45 created by Jericho (T.W.V., also known as "The White Void"). The squadron (before completely disconnecting from the N.E.X. system) gave a brief description of what they saw. (Camera use was completely disabled because the anomaly used some type of radiation to block interference.) They described the following: a kind of enormous monster, over ten meters tall, with several eyes on its stomach and arms, supposedly visible to the naked eye, and "roars that made them tremble to their bones". At 2:45 PM, after a long attempt at re-establishing communication, the squadron declared itself lost and downed in its exploration attempt. Following these events, the Alpha-e-Omega's sister squadron, Beta-e-Zeta, was sent on an annihilation mission. Now better armed than the prior squadron, they fought their way through the 45 "serie". Sadly, upon arrival, the monster was no longer present. Jericho was interrogated shortly afterward, but he didn't mention knowing anything. These events also seem to have passed Nex and Gabriel's eyes. The investigation and completion of the 45 series will continue in future expeditions, or, if needed in the future, will be completely halted, and quarantined due to their mortality rate.

In the hours following the failed recovery operation, the 45 series exhibited irregular behavior inconsistent with standard anomaly decay. Several monitoring stations reported a low-frequency pulse emanating from within the series structure, even though no active entity was visually confirmed. This pulse appeared to synchronize with the heartbeat of nearby personnel, causing nausea, brief memory lapses, and involuntary auditory hallucinations resembling distant chanting. At 3:11 PM, a dormant corridor within the 45 serie briefly reopened on its own, revealing what looked like a secondary chamber that had not appeared on any prior scans. Beta-e-Zeta attempted to secure the area, but the passage collapsed before entry could be completed. One member reported seeing “something standing behind the wall,” though no physical trace remained afterward. The report was logged, but all footage was corrupted by a white static pattern that matched the same interference described by Alpha-e-Omega. Jericho interrogation, like mentioned before, yielded no useful confession, but; Several inconsistencies were noted in his responses. He referred to the 45 series as “unfinished,” and later claimed that “the thing they saw was not the thing that arrived.” When pressed further, he stated only that the anomaly was “drawn in by observation itself,” implying that awareness may have been a trigger rather than a defense. Only to then mention, that the serie 45/series 45 was actually not the actual White Void.No further clarification was obtained.
